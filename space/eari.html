Earth

Classification:

Terrestrial Planet

Discovery & History:

The only known planet with life; studied extensively throughout human history.

Size & Structure:

Diameter of 12,742 km; composed of a solid inner core, liquid outer core, mantle, and crust.

Atmosphere & Climate:

Nitrogen (78%) and oxygen (21%) dominant atmosphere; supports diverse climates and weather patterns.

Orbital Details:

Orbits the Sun in 365.25 days; rotates once every ~24 hours.

Moons & Rings:

One natural satellite, the Moon; no rings.

Notable Features:

The only known planet with liquid water, a stable atmosphere, and diverse ecosystems.

Habitability & Exploration:

The only known habitable planet; extensively explored by human missions and satellites.

Fun Facts:

70% of Earth's surface is covered by water, and it has the perfect conditions to sustain life.
